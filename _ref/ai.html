Structured discovery first — use-case prioritization, data readiness, ROI modeling — before any production work. You get a clear roadmap and a go/no-go decision, not a prototype with no path forward.

Two reasons. We scope integrations, guardrails, and operational handoff at design time, not month six. And most engagements start on top of our pre-engineered AI products — integrating and tuning, not building from zero.
